Welcome to WordWhiz!

WordWhiz is a multilingual word puzzle game that challenges your vocabulary across different languages.

How to Play

  • Guess the word in 6 tries or less
  • Each guess must be a valid word
  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was
  • Green: Correct letter in correct position
  • Yellow: Correct letter in wrong position
  • Gray: Letter not in the word

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the language?

Tap the settings icon and select your preferred language from the dropdown menu.

Can I play offline?

Yes! Once downloaded, WordWhiz can be played without an internet connection.

How are words selected?

Words are randomly selected from our curated dictionary for each language.
